Nicole Johnson, Owner

The inspiration behind Seoul is to create a salon
environment where style and inner and outer beauty together
can make a great experience. After working in corporate, private, chain, and hair restoration salons, I decided it was time to reinvent my personal style from the inside out. With the help of family and friends we’ve made it possible to customize style with color and cuts to enhance personal beauty. Come experience and look into Seoul to combine your own inner and outer beauty. I hope you enjoy Seoul as much as I do!

About me: I’m adopted Korean-American. I went to beauty school and found out that it was my passion. I’ve been in the professional salon industry since 2001. In 2007 I decided to venture off onto my own and open Seoul. I love every aspect of doing hair from the beginning to the end. I’m very experienced in all aspects of the salon industry. I enjoy what I do, and it’s not a job, it’s my passion. I love making my guests feel like a million bucks inside and out! Seeing my guests leave with an extra bounce in their step, there is no price tag for that! I try not to take life too seriously anymore...because it’s better to laugh and have tons of friends!

-Nicole

Buffy Sedlechek

I started my career in the hair industry in 1993.  I walked into the Aveda Institute and knew that this was my calling.  I went back to my corporate job and gave my notice.  Thirty days later, I started my new journey and have never looked back.      I love meeting new clients and creating a new look for them. Styles that I create can start from simply adding bangs to more complex styles such as adding color with foils.  My favorite styles are when a client wants a new cut and color.  I'm all about creating the entire image!      My work experience is not limited. I work with people who are on chemotherapy and need to have their hair clippered or cut into a shorter length.  This is a hard transition and decision for clients and I am honored that my clients ask me to be apart of their journey.      These past 16 years have been wonderful; I look foreward to continuing my journey at SEOUL SALON.

-Jeanie Ferenz
